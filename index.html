A Hackathon For Women By Women


NYU WinC is the largest community of technical women at NYU, with over 200 members. At FlawlessHacks we will bring together over 100 women engineers from the greater New York area and from colleges such as NYU, Columbia, Barnard, Pace, and Cooper Union for a day of coding, mentorship and awesome projects!

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

All undergraduate and graduate students from any school are welcome. If you are under 18, we’ll need a parental consent form.

Of course! It is absolutely not necessary to have a team prior to coming, and we can hook you up with a team when you get here.
